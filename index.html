Ke Wang

Biography

Ke Wang is an undergraduate student at School of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University. He is an incoming Ph.D. student in Multimedia Laboratory (MMLab) at CUHK, supervised by Prof. Hongsheng Li and Prof. Xiaogang Wang.
Currently, he work as a research scientist intern at AIGC Research and Application Group, SenseTime with Researcher Mingjie Zhan. His research interests lie in Large Language Models (LLMs) for Mathematical Reasoning and Large Multimodal Models (LMMs).
